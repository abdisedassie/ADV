Group Story

Our group consists of determined and dynamic individuals that love God and music.

Vision

This group aims to give a helping hand to churches that are limited in music resources.

Audio Services

We provide quality sound system services. Our main goal is to spread the word of God through music.

Music

Each one of one us are commited on serving God and giving back on what he gifted us.

Hello, we are Aliento De Vida.


Our group came to be by the grace of God with the hunger of playing for him and helping young lives to seek God. Most of us have been friends for many years and we have become a family.


We want to give our all to serve God and for his word to be heard in the whole country of Belize. We want to help our youth, for them to see that serving God is the most beautiful thing to do.


In conclusion, by the grace of God, we want to be a band full of the Holy Spirit and be able to attract young people to give their music talents to God.

Our Skills

Our group play a variety of different genres.

We are determined to continue learning and find more of God.
